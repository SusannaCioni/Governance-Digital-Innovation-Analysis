# Digital Transformation in Public Administrations: A Case Study
Project Overview
This project explores the complexities of Digital Innovation within Public Administrations (PPAA). It focuses on the strategic, regulatory, and organizational challenges of implementing ICT solutions in institutional contexts. The analysis bridges the gap between technological potential and administrative governance.

Key Areas of Analysis

Innovation Governance: Examining the decision-making processes behind digital adoption in public systems.

ICT Integration: Analyzing the impact of information technology on administrative efficiency and service delivery.

Regulatory Compliance: Addressing the legal and administrative frameworks (Administrative Law) that govern digital transformation.

Strategic Reporting: Developing data-informed insights to evaluate service innovation and organizational change.

Skills Demonstrated
Public Sector Innovation: Understanding the specific needs of institutional stakeholders.


Complex System Analysis: Identifying barriers to digital transition in large-scale organizations.
